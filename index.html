Our Story

Volt & Wire Electric was founded in 2009 by Marcus Thompson, a third-generation electrician who learned the trade from his grandfather. After working for larger electrical companies and seeing how customers were often treated as just another job number, Marcus decided there had to be a better way.

He started Volt & Wire with a simple mission: provide honest, professional electrical service where safety comes first and customers are treated like family. What began as a one-man operation working out of a pickup truck has grown into Austin's most trusted electrical service company, with a team of skilled electricians serving over 500 customers every year.

We're still family-owned and operated. Marcus personally oversees every job to ensure our high standards are met. We live in the communities we serveâ€”our kids go to your schools, we shop at your stores, and we care about keeping our neighbors safe.
